About Me

Welcome to the digital portfolio of Aaron Key!

As a data professional, I use my expertise in data engineering and data science to make end-to-end advanced analytical solutions tailored to my stakeholders' needs.

I have extensive experience in on-premise data warehousing, using python, SSIS, and SQL to perform ETL processes, and using python for advanced analytics and Power BI and SSRS for self-service reporting.

I have a particular interest in AI solutions, with experience in using pre-trained transformers to address complex analytics problems in unstructured free-text data.

Within my tasks, I take data handling very seriously and work hard to maintain ethical standards and fair use of data, whether the project requires a simple solution or many advanced analytics solutions.
